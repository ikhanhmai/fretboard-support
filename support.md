# Fretboard Support

## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my skill level?**
A: Go to Tools tab > Settings > Skill Level.

**Q: Why can't I hear audio?**
A: Check that your device is not on silent mode and volume is up.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
A: The app is linked to your Apple ID. Re-download from the App Store at no extra cost.
