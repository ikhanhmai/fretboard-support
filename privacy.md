# Privacy Policy — Fretboard

**Last updated: March 2026**

Fretboard does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage
- Practice history and quiz results are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
- Skill level preferences are stored locally in UserDefaults.
- No data leaves your device.

## In-App Purchases
- This app is a paid download. No in-app purchases.

## Analytics & Tracking
- Fretboard does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
- No IDFA or ATT usage.
